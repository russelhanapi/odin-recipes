Gingerbread Fudge

Description

Try this gingerbread fudge for the holidays - you only need a few pantry ingredients and some spices.

Ingredients

  • 2 (11-ounce) packages premium white chocolate chips
  • 1 (14-ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1/4 cup molasses
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 teaspoons ground ginger
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground allspice
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Line an 8x8-inch baking pan with aluminum foil and lightly grease or butter the foil; set aside.
  3. Set aside 1/2 cup white chocolate chips. Combine remaining white chocolate chips, sweetened condensed milk, molasses, and butter in a saucepan set over medium heat. Stir frequently until butter and chocolate chips are completely melted and ingredients are combined.
  4. Add vanilla extract, ginger, cinnamon, nutmeg, allspice, cloves and salt; stir well to combine.
  5. Pour mixture into the prepared pan and smooth out evenly.
  6. Working quickly, microwave the remaining 1/2 cup white chocolate chips in a small microwave-safe bowl until melted, 1 to 1 1/2 minutes, stirring every 30 seconds.
  7. Spoon dollops of the white chocolate mixture atop the fudge and use a knife to gently swirl the 2 mixtures together.
  8. Cover and chill about 2 hours or until firm.
  9. Use foil to lift uncut fudge out of the pan; cut into 1-inch squares. Makes 2 pounds 6 ounces.
